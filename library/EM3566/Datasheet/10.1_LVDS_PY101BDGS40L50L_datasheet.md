1.0 Description
PY101BDGS40L50L is a color active matrix thin film transistor (TFT) liquid
crystal display (LCD) that uses amorphous silicon TFT as a switching device.
It is composed of a TFT LCD panel, Driver IC ,FPC and Backlight.
